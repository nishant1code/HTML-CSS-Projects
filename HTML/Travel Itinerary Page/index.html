Paris Itienrary: Exploring the City in 5 days

Day 1: Arrival

Morning Arrival and hotel check in.

Afternoon Visit the Eiffel Tower and grab some lunch.

Evening Exploring the Louvre Museum and have dinner.

Day 2: Exploring heritage.

Morning Have breakfast and stroll through Montmartre and visit the Sacré-Cœur Basilica.

Afternoon Visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral and Île de la Cité and have lunch.

Evening Enjoy a dinner on the cruise on the Seine River.

Day 3: Palace visit and Opera

Morning Explore the Palace of Versailles after breakfast.

Afternoon Visit the Musée d'Orsay.

Evening Experience a preformance at the Opéra Garnier or explore the Latin Quarter.

Day 4: Exploring City

Morning Discover the neighborhood of Saint-Germain-des-Prés

Afternoon Take a walk along the Canal Saint-Martin.

EveningExplore the Champs-Elysees and visit the Arc de Triomphe.

Day 5: Last Day

Morning Visit the Montparnasse Tower for panoramic view.

Afternoon Explor the steets of Le Marais.

Evening Check out from the hotel and go to the airport.
